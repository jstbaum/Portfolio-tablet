This is Ollie

Ollie is a petite doodle: a mix of a golden retriever, cocker spaniel and poodle. He was born in April of 2019. He is such a sweet boy and loves to meet new people.

Even though Ollie is only 2 years old, he sometimes acts older. He loves laying down and will do anything to take naps and get belly rubs. He is the best older brother to Winnie even though they sometimes fight.
