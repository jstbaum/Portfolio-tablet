This is Winnie

Winnie is a cockapoo: a mix of a cocker spaniel and poodle. She was born in June of 2020. Winnie loves meeting new dogs, swimming, playing fetch and more.

Winnie is the craziest, most energetic dog ever. You can always find her running around the backyard in circles all day. Sometimes we catch Winnie ripping up the mail even though she promises it was Ollie ;)
